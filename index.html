Crypto vs Cash App/Zelle

Not “better for everything” — better for these specific needs.

Cash App / Zelle

  • Easy and familiar
  • U.S.-only, bank-limited
  • Custodial balance
  • Accounts can be frozen

Self-custody wallet

  • Global, 24/7 access
  • You hold your keys
  • No chargebacks
  • Low network fees
